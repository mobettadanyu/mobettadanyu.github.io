KML, KMZ Viewer with Drive
KML(Keyhole Markup Language), KMZ Viewer is a tool that views the .kml, .kmz (Zipped KML format) file in your browser. Free online tool to view KML, KMZ files from the web on a Google map. You can open KML, KMZ files from URL, Google Drive or from your computer.
Provides connect with Google Drive. You can directly open a KML file from Google Drive.
